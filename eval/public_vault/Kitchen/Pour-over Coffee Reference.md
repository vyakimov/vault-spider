# Pour-over Coffee Reference

## Baseline recipe

Use 15 grams of medium-fine coffee and 250 grams of water at roughly 94 degrees Celsius. Rinse the
paper filter, then bloom with 30 grams of water for 30 seconds. Continue with slow pours and aim for
a total drawdown near three minutes.

## Adjustments

If the cup is thin and sour, grind slightly finer. If it is harsh and the drawdown is very slow,
grind coarser. Change only one variable between brews so the result remains interpretable.
