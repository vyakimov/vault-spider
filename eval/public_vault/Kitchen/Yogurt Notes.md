# Yogurt Notes

## Working method

Heat a litre of whole milk to 90°C, hold ten minutes, cool to 45°C, whisk in two tablespoons of
the previous batch, and incubate around 43°C for six to eight hours. Longer incubation trades
mildness for tang.

## Observations

- The hold at 90°C is what sets thickness; skipping it makes drinkable yogurt.
- A tea towel around the pot in the oven with the light on holds temperature well enough.
- Reculturing works for about six generations before the texture drifts and a fresh starter is
  worth buying.
