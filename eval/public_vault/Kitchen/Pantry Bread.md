# Pantry Bread

## Formula

Combine 500 grams of bread flour, 350 grams of water, 10 grams of salt, and 3 grams of instant yeast.
Mix until no dry flour remains, then rest for 20 minutes before a short knead.

## Fermentation and baking

Let the dough rise until roughly doubled, shape it, and proof for about 45 minutes. Bake in a fully
preheated covered pot at 230 degrees Celsius for 25 minutes, then uncover for another 15 minutes.
