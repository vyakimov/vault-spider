# Field Sampling Schedule

| Activity | Cadence | Window |
|---|---|---|
| Soil moisture samples | Weekly | Tuesday at 07:00 during growing season |
| Bird point counts | Weekly | April and May, within 30 minutes of sunrise |
| Weather sensor calibration | Twice yearly | First working Monday of January and July |
| Backup restore drill | Quarterly | Any announced maintenance window |

Weather can move bird counts to the next calm morning. Soil sampling should remain at the scheduled
time so results are comparable across weeks.
