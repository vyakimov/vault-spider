# Mercury Observation Notes

These notes concern the planet Mercury and are unrelated to [[Mercury Data Migration|Project
Mercury]].

## April 2025 session

Mercury was observed shortly after sunset near greatest eastern elongation. A small telescope at
low magnification showed a bright, partially illuminated disk, but atmospheric turbulence near the
horizon limited detail.

## Practical lesson

Begin observing as soon as the sky is dark enough to locate the planet. Waiting for a darker sky
also lets Mercury sink into thicker, less stable air.
