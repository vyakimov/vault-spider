# Soil Moisture Study

## Sampling design

The study compares mulched and unmulched plots at three fictional stations. Manual samples are
taken at depths of 10 centimetres and 30 centimetres so surface drying can be distinguished from
deeper soil conditions.

## Timing

Sampling occurs every Tuesday at 07:00 local time during the growing season. The field sheet also
records rainfall during the previous 24 hours and whether irrigation ran.

## Sensor comparison

The manual gravimetric sample is the reference. Atlas probe readings from the nearest five-minute
interval are joined later by station, depth, and timestamp.
