# Vegetation Survey Protocol

## Method

Fixed one-metre quadrats at each marked station, photographed from directly above with the
frame in shot, then percentage cover estimated per species against the standard chart. The same
observer scores a whole season to keep estimates comparable.

## Timing

Quadrats are scored monthly during the growing season, in the same week as the soil sampling
round so site visits stay consolidated (see [[Field Sampling Schedule]]).

## Records

Species, percent cover, quadrat photo reference, and phenology notes (flowering, seed set).
Unknown plants get a photo and a placeholder code rather than a guessed species.
