# Bird Survey Protocol

## Method

Each survey uses a ten-minute stationary point count beginning within 30 minutes of sunrise. Record
every bird seen or heard within a 100-metre radius, along with broad weather conditions.

## Schedule

Counts occur once a week during April and May. Do not survey during heavy rain or sustained strong
wind; postpone to the next calm morning instead.

## Privacy and disturbance

The public study stores species, count, station, time, and weather only. It does not retain audio,
photographs of visitors, or observer names. Remain on marked paths and do not approach nests.
