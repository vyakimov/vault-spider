# Weather Station Calibration

## Schedule

Temperature and humidity sensors are checked every six months, on the first working Monday of
January and July. Rain gauges receive an additional mechanical inspection each January.

## Temperature check

Use an ice-water bath for the zero-degree point and a certified ambient reference for the second
point. Replace or recalibrate a probe if either reading differs by more than 0.4 degrees Celsius.

## Humidity check

Use a sealed saturated-salt chamber that produces approximately 75 percent relative humidity.
Investigate drift greater than three percentage points.

## Recording

Record the sensor ID, reference value, observed value, adjustment, and next due date. Do not put
operator names in the public calibration record.
