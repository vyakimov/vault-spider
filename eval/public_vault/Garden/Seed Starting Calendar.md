# Seed Starting Calendar

## Indoors under lights

- **Late February**: chillies and aubergines — they need the longest run.
- **Mid March**: tomatoes. Earlier just makes leggy plants that sulk after transplant.
- **Early April**: brassicas and lettuce in modules.

## Direct sown

- **After last frost**: beans, courgettes, and the second lettuce wave straight into the beds
  described in [[Raised Bed Notes]].

## Rule learned the hard way

Label trays with variety *and* sowing date. Two mystery trays of identical seedlings taught
this lesson permanently.
