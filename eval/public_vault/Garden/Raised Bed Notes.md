# Raised Bed Notes

## Dimensions

A bed 1.2 metres wide can be reached from both sides without stepping on the soil. Paths should be
wide enough for a wheelbarrow and should remain separate from the compost storage area.

## Materials

Untreated rot-resistant boards are preferable where available. Avoid painted, pressure-treated, or
unknown salvaged wood around food crops.

## Filling

Use ordinary topsoil blended with mature compost. Large quantities of unfinished compost can settle
and temporarily compete with plants for nitrogen, so keep active composting in a dedicated bin.
