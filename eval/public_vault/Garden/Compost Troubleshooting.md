# Compost Troubleshooting

## Pile is cold

First check moisture. If the pile is dry, add water while turning it. If it is damp but still cold,
mix in nitrogen-rich material such as fresh plant trimmings or coffee grounds. Very small piles may
also lose heat too quickly.

## Pile smells sour

A sour or rotten smell usually means insufficient air or too many wet greens. Turn the pile and mix
in shredded cardboard or dry leaves. Do not solve an air problem by adding more food scraps.

## Coffee-ground crust

Coffee grounds can compact when spread in a thick layer. Break them up and mix them with coarse dry
material rather than leaving a continuous layer.
