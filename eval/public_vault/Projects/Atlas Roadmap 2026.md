# Atlas Roadmap 2026

## Candidate work

- **Solar trial.** Power one field sensor from a small panel and battery through a full winter
  before committing the rest of the fleet.
- **Second site.** Evaluate a second field site on the north ridge; depends on a repeat of the
  coverage survey, not on new hardware.
- **Alert digests.** Fold routine alerts into a daily digest so only actionable pages interrupt.

## Explicitly deferred

- Public data portal. Revisit once the retention summaries have a year of history.
- Replacing [[Atlas Sensor Hub Deployment|the current deployment shape]]; it is boring and works.

## Sequencing

Solar trial first: it is reversible, cheap, and informs the second-site power budget. Nothing on
this list changes the data path described in [[Atlas Sensor Hub Architecture]].
