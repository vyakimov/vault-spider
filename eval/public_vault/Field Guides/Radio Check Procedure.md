# Radio Check Procedure

## Before leaving the vehicle

Both handhelds on channel 2, volume up, one full radio check exchange completed — not just
"can you hear me" but a readability report both ways. Spare battery in the pack that goes with
the person walking furthest.

## During the visit

A check-in on the hour when working out of line of sight. A missed check-in means stop work and
re-establish contact before anything else.

## Back at the vehicle

Radios back in the charging cradle, flat batteries flagged. The kit list lives in
[[Field Equipment Checklist]].
