# Field Equipment Checklist

## Every visit

- Charged field tablet and a paper backup sheet.
- Spare sensor battery and weatherproof connectors.
- Calibration reference probe.
- Flagging tape, ruler, and sample labels.
- Drinking water and basic first-aid kit.

## Gateway maintenance visits

Bring the locked equipment-cabinet key, a USB recovery drive, and a short Ethernet cable. Never
factory-reset Cedar until its SQLite queue has been copied or confirmed empty.

## Before leaving

Count samples, photograph equipment condition without including people, and check that temporary
flags have either been removed or recorded for the next visit.
