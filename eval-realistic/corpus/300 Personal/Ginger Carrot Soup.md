A [[recipe]] for ginger carrot soup


1kg carrots, rough chunks
80g fresh ginger, peeled and sliced thin
2 onions, sautéed until soft
1.5L vegetable stock
1 tin coconut milk (400ml)
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp coriander seed, toasted and ground
salt to taste

sweat onions, add spices until fragrant, add carrots + ginger + stock, simmer 25 min,
blend smooth, stir in coconut milk, don't boil after that
